A look into Europe's economy and demography

Europe is home to many countries with many different cultures and ways of life.

With the European Union, those countries represent one of the global power in international relations.

However, we could wonder how different are the living condition between those countries.

This visualization will aim at giving an overview of various economic and demographic aspects of European countries.

Europe's population and GDP

This map helps us visualize the geography of Europe and locate each countries.

It also gives a idea on how each country compares with one another regarding their GDP and population.

Life Expectancy of European countries against the median income

Life expectancy is clear indicator of living conditions.

It seems that there might be a possible relation between the median income of a country and the average life expectancy.

Also, it seems that countries with lower median income tend to also have a higher unemployment rate.

The dotted line represents the average median income among the European countries.

The relationship of Life Satisfaction, Job Satisfaction and Median Income

Here we see that countries with higher job satisfaction, also have higher life satisfaction.

This relationship seems to be linear.

Again, Median Income seems to also have a relation with both life and job satisfaction.
